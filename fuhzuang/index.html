阿木支系服装

The clothing of the Amu branch

服装特点：女性头戴八角形藏青色包头，用缀银泡的彩带固定，帽子左右两边缀一束红绒线垂至双耳，上衣为右开襟的靛青色上衣，胸前挂银链和银制饰物，下穿靛青色摆裙或长裤，臀后缀腰带

Headwear: Women wear an octagonal dark blue headscarf, secured with a colorful ribbon adorned with silver studs. A bunch of red velvet threads hang from both sides of the headscarf, extending to the ears.Upper Garment: The upper clothing is a right-side fastening indigo jacket, with silver chains and silver accessories hanging on the chest.Lower Garment: The lower clothing consists of an indigo flared skirt or long trousers, with a belt attached at the back.

奕车支系

服装特点：奕车支系的女性服饰较为独特，喜欢穿黑色多层衣，里外套穿可能多达十余件，象征层层梯田。服饰通常以黑色为主色调，搭配银饰和刺绣装饰。

The Yiche branch

The clothing features are as follows:The clothing of the Yiche branch is quite distinctive, especially for women. They prefer to wear multi-layered black clothing, with as many as ten or more layers of jackets worn one over the other, symbolizing the terraced fields. The attire is predominantly black, complemented by silver ornaments and embroidered decorations.

卡多支系

服装特点：耳戴芝麻铃，包头形状如圆盘，用青或黑色布制作，包头外沿刺绣图案并镶嵌银泡，束红、绿绒线绒花插于脑后。上衣是青或蓝色右开襟短衣，下穿靛青色大筒裙，配刺绣围裙、飘带，三颗银制八角花缀在胸前。

The Kaduo branch

Characteristics of the clothing:Earrings: Wearing sesame-shaped bells on the ears.Headwear: The headscarf is shaped like a round disc, made of dark blue or black cloth. The outer edge of the headscarf is embroidered with patterns and adorned with silver studs. Red and green velvet flowers are inserted at the back of the head.Upper Garment: The upper clothing is a short jacket with a right-sidefastening, in dark blue or blue color.Lower Garment: A large indigo skirt is worn, along with an embroidered apron and sashes. Three silver octagonal flowers are attached to the chest.

哈尼支系

服装特点：哈尼支系的服饰款式多样，女性常戴公鸡帽或猫头鹰帽，上衣多为右开襟长衣，颜色以黑色或青色为主，搭配刺绣腰带和大摆裙。

The Hani branch

Characteristics of the clothing:The Hani branch has a diverse range of clothing styles. Women often wear rooster hats or owl hats. The upper garment is usually a long-sleeved jacket with a right-side fastening, predominantly in black or dark blue colors. The outfit is typically complemented with an embroidered waistband and a flared skirt.
