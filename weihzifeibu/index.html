哈尼族人口分布图

Distribution Map of the Hani Population

人口与分布

人口：约166万（2021年数据），在中国少数民族中居第16位。

分布：主要分布在云南省南部红河哈尼族彝族自治州、普洱市、西双版纳傣族自治州及玉溪市。缅甸、老挝、泰国北部也有少量分布。

Population and Distribution

Population: Approximately 1.66 million (data from 2021), ranking 16th among China's ethnic minorities.

Distribution: Mainly distributed in the southern part of Yunnan Province, including the Honghe Hani and Yi Autonomous Prefecture, Pu'er City, Xishuangbanna Dai Autonomous Prefecture, and Yuxi City. There are also small populations in northern Myanmar, Laos, and Thailand.

Language and Script o

语言与文字

语言：属汉藏语系藏缅语族彝语支，分哈雅、碧卡、豪白三大方言。

文字：历史上无通用文字，1957年创制拉丁字母拼音文字，但传统仍多使用口传文化。

Language and Script

Language: It belongs to the Yi branch of the Tibeto-Burman languages, which is part of the Sino-Tibetan language family. It is divided into three major dialects: Ha-Ya, Bi-Ka, and Hao-Bai.

Script: Historically, there was no common written script. In 1957, a phonetic script based on the Latin alphabet was created. However, traditional oral culture remains widely used.

哈尼族历史

History of the Hani People

历史

源于古代羌族南迁，隋唐时期称"和蛮"，明清文献称"窝泥""和泥"，定居滇南山区后形成梯田农耕文化。

History

The group originated from the southern migration of the ancient Qiang people. During the Sui and Tang dynasties, they were referred to as "Huo Man." In the documents of the Ming and Qing dynasties, they were called "Wo Ni" or "He Ni." After settling in the mountainous regions of southern Yunnan, they developed a terraced farming culture.

哈尼族手工业

Hani Handicrafts

经济生活

农业：以梯田稻作闻名（如元阳哈尼梯田为世界文化遗产），兼营玉米、茶叶、甘蔗等。

手工业：擅长染织、竹编和银器制作

Economic Life

Agriculture: Known for terraced rice cultivation (e.g., the Yuanyang Hani Terraces are a UNESCO World Cultural Heritage site), and also engaged in the cultivation of maize, tea, sugarcane, etc.

Handicrafts: Skilled in dyeing and weaving, bamboo weaving, and silverware making.

哈尼族舞蹈

Hani Dance

文化与风俗

服饰：尚黑，女性多穿短衣长裤/裙，配银饰；男性传统对襟上衣、包头巾。

音乐舞蹈：传统乐器有巴乌、三弦；舞蹈如"乐作舞"节奏欢快。

Culture and Customs

Clothing: Prefers black. Women often wear short jackets with long trousers or skirts, accessorized with silver jewelry. Men traditionally wear cross-collared jackets and headscarves.

Music and Dance: Traditional musical instruments include the Bawu and three-stringed lute. Dances such as the "Lezuo Dance" are characterized by their lively rhythms.

哈尼族宗教信仰

Religious Beliefs of the Hani People

宗教信仰

原始宗教为主，崇拜自然、祖先和神灵。"贝玛"（祭司）主持祭祀，部分地区受佛教或基督教影响。

Religious Beliefs

Primarily based on animistic beliefs, with worship of nature, ancestors, and spirits. The "Bimaw" (priest) presides over rituals and ceremonies. In some regions, there is influence from Buddhism or Christianity.

哈尼族长街宴

The Long Street Banquet of the Hani People

特色文化

梯田文明：哈尼梯田体现人与自然和谐共生，2013年列入世界文化遗产。

长街宴：节日时全村聚餐，数百米宴席象征团结。

Characteristic Culture

Terrace Civilization: The Hani terraces exemplify the harmonious coexistence of humans and nature. They were inscribed on the UNESCO World Heritage List in 2013.

Long Street Banquet: During festivals, the entire village gathers for a communal feast. The banquet tables can stretch for hundreds of meters, symbolizing unity.
