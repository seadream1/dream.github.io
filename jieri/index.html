十月年

October New Year

十月年：这是哈尼族最重要的节日，相当于汉族的春节。

哈尼族以农历十月为岁首，所以在农历十月庆祝新年。节期一般为五六天，有的地方甚至长达半个月。在这个节日里，哈尼族人要打扫房屋，换上新衣，杀猪宰牛，舂粑粑，做团籽面，祭祀祖先和寨神，互相拜访赠礼，回娘家探亲，举行荡秋千、摔跤、陀螺、磨秋等游戏活动，晚上围着篝火唱歌跳舞，载歌载舞，欢度新年。

The October New Year: This is the most important festival of the Hani people, equivalent to the Han Chinese Spring Festival.

The Hani people consider the tenth month of the lunar calendar as the beginning of the year, so they celebrate the New Year during that time. The festival typically lasts five or six days, and in some places, it can extend up to half a month. During this holiday, the Hani people clean their houses, put on new clothes, slaughter pigs and cattle, pound glutinous rice cakes, make round rice balls, and offer sacrifices to their ancestors and the village god. They also visit each other to exchange gifts, return to their maternal homes for reunions, and engage in games such as swinging, wrestling, spinning tops, and playing on a seesaw-like swing. At night, they gather around bonfires, singing and dancing to celebrate the New Year with joy and festivity.

六月年节日

June Festival

六月年：这是哈尼族的农业祭祀节日，也叫苦扎扎节或火把节。

一天，哈尼族人要杀牲祭祀竜树（竜神的象征），点燃火把游行村寨，烧掉田间的草木和虫子，还要在田间放鞭炮和敲锣打鼓，制造响声来吓走害兽。晚上，在村中心广场举行盛大的歌舞会，男女青年互相表达爱意。

June Festival: This is an agricultural sacrificial festival of the Hani ethnic group, also known as the Kuzhazha Festival or Torch Festival.

On this day, the Hani people sacrifice livestock to worship the sacred Dragon Tree (a symbol of the Dragon Deity), light torches for processions through the village to burn weeds and insects in the fields, and set off firecrackers while beating gongs and drums to create loud noises that scare away harmful beasts.In the evening, a grand singing and dancing gathering is held at the village square, where young men and women express affection for one another.

吃新谷节日

Tasting the New Grain

吃新谷：这是哈尼族在每年农历二月初八或初九举行的一个小型节日，是为了庆祝新粮上市。

在这一天，哈尼族人要用新米蒸饭或做粑粑，并用新米酿制美酒，请亲友邻居共享美食佳酿。同时，也要用新米和鸡蛋等祭品祭拜寨神和田神，感谢他们赐予丰收，并祈求来年五谷丰登。

Tasting the New Grain: This is a minor festival celebrated by the Hani people on the eighth or ninth day of the second lunar month each year, marking the arrival of the new harvest.

On this day, the Hani people cook rice or make rice cakes with the newly harvested grain and brew wine from the fresh rice to share with relatives, friends, and neighbors. They also offer sacrifices of new rice, eggs, and other offerings to the village deity and the field deity, expressing gratitude for the bountiful harvest and praying for another prosperous year of abundant crops.

姑娘节节日

Maiden's Festival

姑娘节：这是哈尼族女性的专属节日，在每年农历三月十五举行。

这一天是女性休息和娱乐的日子，男性要承担所有家务和农活，并给女性送上礼物和美食。女性则可以自由地外出游玩、走亲访友、唱歌跳舞、交友谈情。这个节日体现了哈尼族对女性的尊重和关爱。

Maiden's Festival: This is an exclusive celebration for Hani women, observed annually on the 15th day of the third lunar month.

On this day, women enjoy a day of rest and recreation while men take over all household chores and farm work, presenting gifts and delicacies to the women. The female participants are free to go outing, visit relatives and friends, sing and dance, or socialize with potential partners. This festival embodies the Hani people's respect and care for women.

敬老节日

Respect for the Aged Festival

敬老节：这是哈尼族对老人表示敬意和孝心的节日，在每年农历九月初九举行。

这一天，哈尼族人要给老人洗脚、梳头、按摩、剪指甲等，还要给老人送上粑粑、鸡蛋、糖果等食物，并祝福老人健康长寿。老人则要给晚辈讲述哈尼族的历史和传说，传授哈尼族的文化和智慧。这个节日体现了哈尼族的敬老爱幼和传承文化的传统。

Respect for the Aged Festival: This is a Hani ethnic festival to honor and show filial piety to the elderly, celebrated annually on the ninth day of the ninth lunar month.

On this day, the Hani people wash the elderly's feet, comb their hair, give massages, trim their nails, and present them with traditional foods like rice cakes, eggs, and candies while wishing them good health and longevity. In return, the elders share Hani history, legends, and cultural wisdom with the younger generations. This festival embodies the Hani tradition of respecting the elderly, cherishing the young, and preserving their cultural heritage.
